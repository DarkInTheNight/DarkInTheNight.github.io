About Me

My name is Behruzbek Ahmedov, and I am a Year 12 student at St. Paul's School, London. I began AralSTEM in order to boost STEM education for Central Asian children after I noticed the lack of a practical education in the sciences in my the region where my parents grew up in Uzbekistan.

All donations will be used towards funding a hands-on science education for children in Central Asian schools.
