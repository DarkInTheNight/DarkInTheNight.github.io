Water

In today's lesson, I taught the kids about the various properties of water, such as cohesiveness and adhesiveness, capillary action, and surface tension. We finished the lesson by giving the kids a chance to experiment with giant bubbles.

The lesson began with an explanation of water's properties.

We used surface tension to explain bubble behaviour.

The kids then got to try out a mixture that created huge bubbles!

All of the kids gave it their best shot.

And this was the most impressive effort!
